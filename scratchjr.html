Introduction to Scratch Jr

Welcome to Scratch Jr, a simplified version of Scratch designed for younger children.

Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Download and install the Scratch Jr app on your device.
  2. Open the app and explore the interface.
  3. Create your first Scratch Jr project with colorful characters and backgrounds.
  4. Add blocks to make characters move, dance, and interact.
